FOR MORE THAN A CENTURY,

The Summit Country Day School has been a beacon of excellence in Catholic education.
Located in midtown Cincinnati, we are an independent,coeducational, college-preparatory school with a Montessori preschool and divisions through grade 12. Our mission is to develop leaders of character through superior academic and spiritual growth.
